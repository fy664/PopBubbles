Place your sound effect files here in .wav or .ogg format.

Recommended files:
  pop.wav       - Bubble pop sound
  hurt.wav      - Player hurt sound
  click.wav     - UI button click
  hover.wav     - UI button hover
  gameover.wav  - Game over jingle
  shoot.wav     - Shooter bubble firing
  music.ogg     - Background music (looped)

After adding files, uncomment the load lines in src/core/Audio.cpp.
Free sound resources: https://freesound.org/  https://opengameart.org/
